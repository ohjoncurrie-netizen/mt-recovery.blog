background & context

as a community of educators and neighbors, we believe that recovery begins with understanding. the current fentanyl crisis in montana is influenced by shifting supply routes and the arrival of synthetic adulterants.

"history is not just a study of the past, but a map of how we survive the present."

this project serves as a technical bridge between public safety records and life-saving rehabilitation. by centralizing these resources, we aim to decrease the barrier to entry for those seeking a path forward.

built in montana.
